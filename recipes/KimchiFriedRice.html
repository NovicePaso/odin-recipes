Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi Fried Rice is a traditional korean dish passed down from generation to generation with the purpose of bringing together family, culture and generations alike. This recipe is one that has been taken from a variety of sources and added my own spin on it, as a Japanese-Chinese-American. There are many ways to enjoy this incredibly versatile dish to fit your favorite ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Rice. Day old refridgerated rice works best
  • Kimchi
  • Protein (such as spam, pork belly, bulgogi, fish cake, etc)
  • Green Onion
  • Gochujang
  • Sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds
  • Nori (garnish)
  • Fried Egg

Steps

  1. Fry the Kimchi over high heat
  2. Fry the meat of your choice
  3. Lower the heat to medium-high. Add rice
  4. add Gochujang, Sesame oil to taste. Mix well
  5. Remove from heat, add chopped green onions, sesame seeds, nori and fried egg
