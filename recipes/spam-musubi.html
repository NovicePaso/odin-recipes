Spam Musubi

Description

Spam Musubi is a quick and easy snack that can act as a full meal or something in-between. You can add radish, Egg, Perilla leaf, etc to keep your taste buds fresh and engaged! Who doesn't like spam musubi?!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Spam
  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Nori Sheets

Steps

  1. Cut the spam into long sheets
  2. Fry the spam for a nice crispy surface
  3. Put the fried spam into a bowl of the teriyaki sauce to marinade
  4. Make a layer of rice and place into the spam musubi mold. Ensure the layer of rice is even and the amount to your liking
  5. Press the top of the mold onto the spam and rice to give it the rectangular shape
  6. Cut the nori into strips. Note, you can adjust the width of the sheets for different aesthetics!
  7. Remove the mold and wrap the nori strips onto the different sections of the musubi
  8. Cut the musubi into lengths of your choice
  9. Enjoy!
